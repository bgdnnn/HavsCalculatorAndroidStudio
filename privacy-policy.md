Privacy Policy – HAVS Calculator

HAVS Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app operates entirely offline.
All data entered by the user (such as tool information and calculation history) is stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

The app does not use:

Analytics services

Advertising services

Tracking technologies

User accounts

Internet connections
